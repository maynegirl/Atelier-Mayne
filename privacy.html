Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Atelier Mayne, Mumbai, India

This Privacy Policy is compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). It explains what personal data Atelier Mayne collects, why, how it is used, and the rights you hold over your data.

1. Who We Are

Atelier Mayne is a boutique interior design practice founded by Anjali Mayne, based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. We operate this website at www.ateliermayne.com to present our services and receive commission enquiries.

For the purposes of the DPDP Act, 2023, Atelier Mayne is the Data Fiduciary — the entity that determines the purpose and means of processing your personal data.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it to us. This includes:

  • Contact form submissions: Your name, email address, nature of project, and any details you share in your message
  • Email correspondence: Any personal information included in emails you send to hello@ateliermayne.com
  • WhatsApp enquiries: Your name and message content if you contact us via our WhatsApp number

We do not collect payment information, government identification, or sensitive personal data of any kind through this website. We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools on this website.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

Your personal data is collected and processed solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your commission enquiry or question
  • To assess whether your project is a suitable fit for our practice
  • To communicate with you regarding a potential or ongoing commission

We collect your data on the basis of your free, specific, informed, and voluntary consent — given when you submit our contact form or write to us directly. You may withdraw this consent at any time (see Section 6).

4. How We Use and Store Your Data

Your data is used only for the purposes stated above. We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Enquiry data is stored securely in our internal correspondence records. If a commission does not proceed, we retain your contact details for a period of 12 months from the date of last correspondence, after which it is deleted. If a commission is confirmed, project-related records are retained for 7 years for professional and legal purposes.

Our contact form is processed via EmailJS, a third-party email delivery service. EmailJS transmits your form submission to our inbox and does not store your data beyond the transmission. Please refer to EmailJS's Privacy Policy for details of their data handling.

5. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

As a Data Principal under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You may request a summary of the personal data we hold about you and the purposes for which it is being processed.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold be corrected or updated.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request that we delete your personal data. We will honour this request unless retention is required for legal or professional reasons.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: You may raise a grievance regarding the processing of your personal data, which we will address within a reasonable time.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to the processing of your data at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.
  • Right to Nominate: You may nominate another individual to exercise your rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity.

6. How to Exercise Your Rights or Withdraw Consent

To exercise any of the rights above, or to withdraw consent to the processing of your personal data, please contact us directly:

  • Email: hello@ateliermayne.com
  • Subject line: Data Privacy Request — [Your Name]

We will acknowledge your request within 7 business days and resolve it within 30 days. There is no fee for making a data request.

7. Grievance Redressal & Data Protection Board

If you are not satisfied with our response to a privacy concern, you have the right to file a complaint with India's Data Protection Board, established under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Information on filing a complaint will be made available by the Government of India upon the Board's operationalisation.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. Our website is served over HTTPS. Our email communications are handled through standard encrypted email infrastructure.

In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights, we will notify you and the relevant authority in accordance with applicable law.

9. Children's Data

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at hello@ateliermayne.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The date of the most recent revision is displayed at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please write to us:
